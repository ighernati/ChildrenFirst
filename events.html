Erasing Family Documentary

Thursday, September 19, 2019

3391 Middlefield Road
Palo Alto, CA 94306
6:00PM - 8:00PM


CHILDREN'S NEEDS FIRST will be hosting a private pre-screening of Erasing Family Documentary with film Director Ginger Gentle.

Erasing Family captures the unheard voices in separation and divorce - the children. Director Ginger Gentle has revealed the trauma experienced by children when they are forced to pick sides, reulting in a loving parent erased from their lives. Family bond obstruction is one of the most significant public health crises of our times. In fact, in the United States, there are an estimated 22 million parents affected by this plight.

The film follows young people as they seek to reunited with their erased families. We hope that their journey will inspire other families to reunite. Resource-rich information packets will be available for audience members at this event. These packets give practical tips for children, parents, and professionals.
