WHO WE ARE



Here at CHILDREN'S NEEDS FIRST, we believe children become ill at any age if they have to live a life centered in mistrust, instrumentalization, and manipulation of relations. Our children need to grow in trust, mutual care, love, acceptance, cooperation with BOTH PARENTS to become well-integrated individuals and social beings

Information

We have the duty to inform when a society fails to recognize that children needs both parents in their life.
